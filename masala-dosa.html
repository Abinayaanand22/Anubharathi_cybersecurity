Masala Dosa

Ingredients

  • 2 cups dosa batter
  • 2 potatoes (boiled)
  • 1 onion (sliced)
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 green chilli
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste

Procedure

  • Heat oil in a pan and add mustard seeds.
  • Add onion and chilli and sauté.
  • Add mashed potatoes and salt.
  • Spread dosa batter on hot pan.
  • Place potato filling inside.
  • Fold dosa and serve hot.
