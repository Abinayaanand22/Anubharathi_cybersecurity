Chole

Ingredients

  • 1 cup chickpeas
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1 onion
  • 1 tsp ginger garlic paste
  • 1 tsp chole masala
  • Salt

Procedure

  • Boil chickpeas until soft.
  • Sauté onions and ginger garlic paste.
  • Add tomato puree.
  • Add chickpeas and spices.
  • Cook for 10 minutes.
  • Serve hot.
