Aloo Paratha

Ingredients

  • 2 cups wheat flour
  • 2 boiled potatoes
  • 1 tsp chilli powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Salt
  • Oil or ghee

Procedure

  • Knead wheat flour into dough.
  • Mash potatoes with spices.
  • Stuff mixture into dough ball.
  • Roll into flat bread.
  • Cook on pan with ghee.
  • Serve hot.
