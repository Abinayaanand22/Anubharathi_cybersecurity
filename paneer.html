Paneer Butter Masala

Ingredients

  • 200g paneer cubes
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 onion
  • 1 tsp ginger garlic paste
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste

Procedure

  • Heat butter and sauté onions.
  • Add ginger garlic paste.
  • Add tomato puree and cook well.
  • Add spices and paneer cubes.
  • Cook for 5 minutes.
  • Serve hot with naan or rice.
