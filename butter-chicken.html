Butter Chicken

Ingredients

  • 250g chicken
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1 onion
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp chilli powder
  • Salt

Procedure

  • Cook chicken pieces in a pan.
  • Sauté onion in butter.
  • Add tomato puree and spices.
  • Add cooked chicken.
  • Cook for 10 minutes.
  • Serve hot.
