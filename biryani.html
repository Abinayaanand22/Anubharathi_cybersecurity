Vegetable Biryani

Ingredients

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 1 carrot (chopped)
  • ½ cup beans
  • ½ cup peas
  • 1 onion (sliced)
  • 1 tsp ginger garlic paste
  • 1 tsp biryani masala
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • 2 cups water
  • Salt to taste

Procedure

  • Heat oil in a cooker and sauté onions until golden.
  • Add ginger garlic paste and cook for 1 minute.
  • Add chopped vegetables and biryani masala.
  • Add washed rice and mix gently.
  • Pour water and add salt.
  • Cook for 2 whistles.
  • Fluff the rice and serve hot.
